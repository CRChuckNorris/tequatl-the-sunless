Welcome to my website

This website is a coursework project for the MSc E-commerce module at Cardiff University. Sorry if you stumbled upon this looking to buy and download the games I have shown here, but all items are just placeholders and I am not selling any actual products.

If you are interested in how I have created the website, I primarily used the python flask framework, which gave a high level of control in every aspect of the website, in the back and front end. For styling I have used Twitter's bootstrap css, which is freely available for use, I would recommend it, as it allows some really cool things just with html and css, including the carousel on the home page.

I wish I had managed my time better during it's creation, I had a lot bigger plans for the entire thing, but with multiple deadlines and other responsibilities I have not been able to work on this I much I had hoped. Nevertheless hope you enjoy exploring the website as much as I did during the time I spent making it!

Thomas Preston
